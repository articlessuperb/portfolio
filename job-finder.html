Remote job finder

Private search for remote junior developer jobs that match my CV. Filters out listings asking for commercial experience or a degree. Password required — not linked from the public site.

Tap search to load live remote listings.
On your iPhone
  1. Open Safari and go to tgollogly.dev/job-finder.html
  2. Enter the password when prompted (username can be anything — only the password is checked)
  3. Tap Search remote jobs
  4. Optional: Share → Add to Home Screen — Safari remembers the password

Set JOB_FINDER_PASSWORD in your Cloudflare Pages secrets if you have not already.
